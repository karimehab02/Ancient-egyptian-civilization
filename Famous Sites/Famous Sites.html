THE GREAT PYRAMIDS OF GIZA

Architectural Marvels

The Giza pyramid complex includes the Great Pyramid of Khufu, the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure, along with the Great Sphinx. Built during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom, these structures have stood for over 4,500 years.

TEMPLES OF LUXOR

The World's Greatest Open-Air Museum

Luxor, ancient Thebes, was the capital of Egypt during the New Kingdom. The city contains some of the most spectacular temples and monuments, including Karnak Temple, Luxor Temple, and the Valley of the Kings.

  • Karnak Temple Complex
  • Luxor Temple
  • Memnon Colossi
  • Hatshepsut's Temple

Abu Simbel Temples

The magnificent rock-cut temples built by Ramses II, famously relocated in the 1960s to save them from the rising waters of Lake Nasser.
